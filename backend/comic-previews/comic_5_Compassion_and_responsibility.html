Compassion and responsibility

Story ID: 5 | Language: English | Difficulty: beginner | Category: test-comic
A 4-scene story about Compassion and responsibility featuring child and animal

Story Overview

A young girl named Anna finds a stray kitten in the park and decides to take it home, learning about compassion and responsibility along the way.

Panel 1
Anna is walking through a park on a sunny day, wearing a backpack. She notices a small, fluffy kitten hiding under a bench, looking scared and alone.
Panel 2
Anna gently approaches the kitten, offering it a piece of her sandwich. The kitten hesitantly comes out, sniffing the food, while Anna smiles warmly.
Panel 3
Anna carries the kitten in her arms, walking towards her house. The kitten looks comfortable and secure, snuggling into her arms. Birds are chirping around them.
Panel 4
Anna arrives home and sets up a cozy bed for the kitten in her room. The kitten curls up and falls asleep, while Anna sits nearby, looking happy and content.
